About

I was born in Sweden in 1994. I was raised in the small city of Borlänge until I was 18 years old when I moved to Linköping to study Computer Engineering at Linköping Technical University. Towards the end of my student years I grew interested in entrepreneurship and mobile development. In Spring 2017 I spent a semester abroad studying at Harbin Institute of Technology in Harbin, China. I wrote my master's thesis during the Spring of 2018. It was about adding gamification elements to an education app for children with learning disabilities to increase their motivation. I graduated in 2018 with a double master's degree from Linköping Technical University and Harbin Institute of Technicology. After that I moved to Stockholm to start working at Meepo AB, a small app-studio. In 2016 I went vegan. In December 2019 I moved to Munich. I want to combine my love for technology with making the world a better place.
