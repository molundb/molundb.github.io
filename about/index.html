About Me

Biography

I was born in Sweden in 1994 and raised in the small city of Borlänge. When I was 18 years old I moved to Linköping to study Computer Engineering at Linköping Technical University. In the Spring of 2017 I spent a semester abroad at Harbin Institute of Technology in China. I graduated in 2018 with a double master's degree from Linköping Technical University and Harbin Institute of Technology. After my studies I moved to Stockholm to work at Meepo, a small app-studio. At Meepo I developed a Flutter app for the charity organization The Life You Can Save. In December 2019 I moved to Munich, Germany to start working at Netlight Consulting, a Swedish IT-consulting company. In March 2021 I made a transfer to Netlight's Berlin office, and that's where I've been living ever since.



Hobbies

I enjoy app development, which is what I do for a living. One of my main goals in life, however, is making the world a better place. I am very interested in what it means to live a good life. The philosophy of life I identify the most with is stoicism. I love music, dancing and going to clubs, concerts, music festivals. I like learning languages and speak German on a C1 level. I want to inspire people by living an exciting, entertaining and fullfilling life while causing as little suffering to the planet, other humans and animals as possible. I have been vegan since June 2016 and like writing reviews for restaurants that I visit. I consider myself an effective altruist and have donated 10 % of my salary to the most effective charities in the world since August 2018.
