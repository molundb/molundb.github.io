About

Biography

I was born in Sweden in 1994 and raised in the small city of Borlänge. When I was 18 years old I moved to Linköping to study Computer Engineering at Linköping Technical University. In the Spring of 2017 I spent a semester abroad studying at Harbin Institute of Technology in Harbin, China. I wrote my master's thesis during the Spring of 2018. It was about adding gamification elements to an education app for children with learning disabilities, in order to increase their motivation to use the app. I graduated in 2018 with a double master's degree from Linköping Technical University and Harbin Institute of Technology. Immediately after my studies I moved to Stockholm to start working at Meepo, a small app-studio. At Meepo I developed a Flutter app for the charity organization The Life You Can Save. In December 2019 I moved to Munich to start working at Netlight Consulting, a Swedish IT-consulting company. In March 2021 I made a transfer to Netlight's Berlin office.

Hobbies

I enjoy mobile development, which is what I do for a living. My main goal in life, however, is making the world a better place. I want to inspire people by living an exciting, entertaining and fullfilling life while causing as little suffering to the planet, other humans and animals as possible. I care a lot about the environment and haven't flown since February 2018. I've been vegan since June 2016. I am an effective altruist and have donated 10 % of my salary to the most effective charities in the world since August 2018.
