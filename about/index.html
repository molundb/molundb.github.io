About Me

Biography

I was born in Sweden in 1994 and raised in the small city of Borlänge. When I was 18 years old I moved to Linköping to study Computer Engineering at Linköping Technical University. In the Spring of 2017 I spent a semester abroad at Harbin Institute of Technology in China. I graduated in 2018 with a double master's degree from Linköping Technical University and Harbin Institute of Technology. After my studies I moved to Stockholm to work at Meepo, a small app-studio. At Meepo I developed a Flutter app for the charity organization The Life You Can Save. In December 2019 I moved to Munich, Germany to start working at Netlight Consulting, a Swedish IT-consulting company. In March 2021 I made a transfer to Netlight's Berlin office, and that's where I've been living ever since.



Hobbies

I am very interested in what it means to live a good life. Stoicism is the life philosophy I resonate with most. My aspiration is to live an exciting, entertaining and fullfilling life while causing as little suffering as possible. I enjoy learning languages and speak Swedish, English, German and basic Spanish and French. I like chess and won the Swedish Chess Championship for kids in 2007 when I was 13 years old. I love music and to play the guitar. I have been vegan since June 2016 and write reviews for restaurants that I visit. I consider myself an effective altruist and one of my main goals in life is making the world a better place. In 2018, I took the Giving What We Can Pledge, and have since been donating 10 % of my salary to the most effective charities in the world.
