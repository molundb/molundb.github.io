Donations

I've calculated that I am part of the richest 1,7 % of the world population before donating 10 % of my income, and part of the richest 2,2 % after donating 10 %. Combined with the fact of how much good donating to effective charities can do, I find that this makes it a moral obligation for me to donate some, non-negligible part of my income. In August 2018 I therefore took the Giving What We Can Pledge to donate 10 % of my after-tax income.

With this page I want to inspire others to also donate money effectively. The world would be unimaginably much better if everyone donated 10 % of their income to effective charities.
