Donations

Here you'll find information about all the considerable donations I've made in my life.

I've calculated that I am part of the richest 1,7 % of the world population before donating 10 % of my income, and part of the richest 2,2 % after donating 10 %. In my eyes this fact, combined with how much good donating to effective charities can do, make it a moral obligation to donate some percentage of my income. In August 2018 I took the Giving What We Can Pledge to donate 10 %.

With this page I want to inspire others to also donate money effectively. The world would be so much better if everyone donated 10 % of their income to effective charities.
