Q4 2019 -

Android Developer - Netlight / Jochen Schweizer My Days

Jochen Schweizer Mydays Group is the market leader of experience products in Germany. The company sells around 1 million vouchers and tickets per year for all types of experiences.

I played a vital part in a small Android team in revamping the app from a state of emergency to a state of the art application written entirely in Kotlin and using MVI as an architectural pattern and the newest technologies and tools such as Room as a local database, Dagger Hilt for dependency injection, coroutines for threading, LiveData and Flow for reactive state management, and the Android navigation component for navigation. More recently we rewrote the entire UI of the app using Android Compose.



Q2 2019 - Q4 2019

Java Developer - Meepo / Itello

Itello is the leading provider of insurance management systems in the Nordics. Their systems need to reliably and securely handle an enormous amount of insurances and transactions each day.

I worked as a Java developer in one of the Scrum teams. I learned what it's like to work in a large organization with a rigurous software development methodology. I became a much better software developer in general and Java developer in particular.



Q3 2018 - Q2 2019

Flutter developer - Meepo / The Life You Can Save

The Life You Can Save is a non-profit organization which analyzes charities and promotes the most effective charities in the world.

As one of two lead developers I was part of building the app from scratch to a release a couple of months later. At the start of the project I was part of deciding the system architecture and setting up the CI/CD pipelines using Fastlane and Jenkins. After that most of my time was spent on flutter development and creating cloud functions in Firebase. I also performed a lot of other tasks, such as being part of customer meetings and planning and doing user testing.
